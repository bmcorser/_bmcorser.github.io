Fun with permutations
=====================

Let :maths:`S = \{1, 2, 3\}` and let :maths:`\alpha : S \rightarrow S` be
the bijection given by :maths:`\alpha(1) = 1`, :maths:`\alpha(2) = 3` and
:maths:`\alpha(3) = 2`

.. maths::

    $
    S = \{1, 2, 3\} \\
    \\
    \alpha : S \rightarrow S \\
    \\
    \alpha =
    \bigl(
        \begin{smallmatrix}
            1 & 2 & 3 \\
            1 & 3 & 2 \\
        \end{smallmatrix}
    \bigr)
    $
